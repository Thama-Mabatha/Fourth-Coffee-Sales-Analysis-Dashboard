# Fourth-Coffee-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze sales performance for a fictional coffee shop chain — Fourth Coffee. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that could support business decisions and improve sales strategies.

Project Overview

Imported and cleaned sales data (CSV) in Power BI.

Designed a modern, data-driven dashboard featuring:

Key Metrics: Total revenue, number of sales, and average revenue per transaction.

Sales Trends: Revenue by month and store location.

Customer Patterns: Revenue by time bucket (morning, afternoon, evening).

Product Insights: Total sales by product category.

Business Insights Panel: Key takeaways summarizing performance trends.

Key Insights

Morning hours (8 AM – 11 AM) generate over 50% of total revenue, identifying a “Morning Rush” business pattern.

Chai Tea and Hot Chocolate Late are the top-selling products.

The majority of sales occur Monday–Friday, indicating strong weekday traffic.

Tools & Technologies

Power BI: Dashboard creation, data visualization, and modeling.

Microsoft Excel: Data preparation and initial exploration.

Data Source: CSV file containing monthly sales and product details.

Outcome

A clean, professional dashboard highlighting sales trends, time-based performance, and top-performing product categories — ideal for presentation to business stakeholders or management.
